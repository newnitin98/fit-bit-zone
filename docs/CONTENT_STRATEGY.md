# CONTENT_STRATEGY.md — Fit Bit Zone Gym

## Content Mission

Create content that makes a first-time visitor feel: "This is a serious gym. I want to train here."

---

## Voice & Tone

### Primary Tone
Energetic, direct, confident, motivating

### Secondary Tone
Authentic, local, approachable, community-oriented

### Avoid
- Corporate / generic template language
- Medical claims or guaranteed result language
- Overly aggressive / intimidating language (gym should feel welcoming)
- Made-up statistics or awards

---

## Headline Strategy

### Hero Headline Formula
[Place Identity] + [Fitness Identity] = Bold aspirational statement
- "Jabalpur's Hardcore Fitness Hub"
- "Transform Your Body at Fit Bit Zone"
- "Where Jabalpur Trains Hard"

### Section Headline Formula
[Action Word] + [Audience Benefit] + [Brand Differentiator]
- "Built for People Who Take Fitness Seriously"
- "Train With a Clear Purpose"
- "8 Reasons to Train With Us"

---

## Section-by-Section Content Notes

### Hero
- Primary: Location identity + fitness identity
- Secondary: Trust signals (stars, reviews) in badge form
- CTA pair: "Join Now" (internal) + "WhatsApp Us" (direct conversion)
- Should feel cinematic and bold

### Stats Section
- 5 numbers that build instant trust
- Each number has a label and a human sub-label
- Design: dark cards with yellow accent icons

### About Section
- Story: "Built for people who take fitness seriously"
- Tone: Authentic, local, non-corporate
- Visuals: Floating stat cards (4.8, 95+) reinforce the copy
- Instagram handle placed naturally in the section

### Programs Section
- 8 cards, each solving a different customer problem
- Short, benefit-first descriptions
- 3 cards marked as "highlight" (Strength, Personal Training, Transformation) draw eye

### Why Choose Us
- 8 reasons — numerical cards
- Short, punchy benefit statements
- Good for overcoming hesitation and objections

### Gallery
- Instagram-inspired grid layout
- Placeholder blocks with gradient + pattern differentiation
- Labels: Strength Zone, Client Love, Bench Press, Gym Love, Transformation Journey, Workout Reels, Training Floor, Fitness Community
- Final note: Replace placeholders with real photos in production

### Membership Section
- 4 plans: Beginner, Strength, Transformation, Personal Training
- No prices — "Enquire on WhatsApp" as CTA
- "Strength Plan" is highlighted as the most popular
- Each plan has: tagline, target audience, features, CTA

### Reviews Section
- 6 cards with star ratings
- Copy is display sample content — clearly disclaimed
- Global 4.8 rating badge shown prominently

### Location Section
- Animated pin on a designed map placeholder
- Full address displayed cleanly
- Two actions: Get Directions + WhatsApp

### Final CTA
- Most emotional section — "Ready to Start Your Fitness Journey?"
- Two CTAs: WhatsApp + Instagram
- Cinematic background treatment
