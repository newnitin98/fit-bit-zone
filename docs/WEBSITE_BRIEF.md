# WEBSITE_BRIEF.md — Fit Bit Zone Gym

## Project Overview

**Client:** Fit Bit Zone Gym (local gym, Jabalpur, Madhya Pradesh)
**Project Type:** Premium Demo / Concept Website
**Purpose:** Demonstrate what a professional digital presence could look like for the gym, with the intent of converting this demo into a production website

---

## Website Goal

1. **Impress the gym owner** — show what premium digital presence looks like
2. **Establish trust** — display ratings, reviews, community features
3. **Drive WhatsApp enquiries** — every section should guide visitors toward contacting on WhatsApp
4. **Improve discoverability** — SEO foundations for local Jabalpur/Shakti Nagar searches
5. **Showcase gym identity** — reflect the hardcore, energetic culture of the gym

---

## Target Audience Personas

### Persona 1: The Beginner (Rahul, 21)
- First-time gym-goer, nervous, doesn't know where to start
- Wants a friendly, supportive environment
- Needs: reassurance, beginner program info, easy contact method

### Persona 2: The Serious Trainer (Ankit, 26)
- Already lifts, looking for a better gym in Jabalpur
- Wants: equipment info, bodybuilding programs, community vibe
- Needs: social proof, program cards, "hardcore" messaging

### Persona 3: The Transformer (Priya, 28)
- Wants to lose weight and transform her body
- Needs: transformation program info, before/after proof, personal training option
- Contact preference: WhatsApp for ease
